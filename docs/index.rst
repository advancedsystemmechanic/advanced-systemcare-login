How to Login Within the Advanced SystemCare Application
=======================================================

The most convenient way to log in is directly from the Advanced SystemCare software interface. Here is a detailed walkthrough of the process:

Step 1: Launch the Application
------------------------------

Begin by opening Advanced SystemCare on your computer. You can find it in the Start Menu (Windows), Applications folder (macOS), or by double-clicking the desktop shortcut if you created one during installation. The main dashboard will appear, displaying system status indicators and available tools.

Step 2: Access the Login Interface
----------------------------------

Look for the account or profile icon, which is typically located in the upper right corner of the main window. In most versions, this appears as a person silhouette or three horizontal dots (⋮) representing the options menu. Click this icon to open a dropdown menu.

From the dropdown menu, select **My Subscription** or **Account**. This action opens the License Manager or Account window, where you can view your current license status and begin the login process.

Alternatively, if you are using a free version, you may see a prominent **Login** or **Sign In** button on the main dashboard, encouraging you to activate premium features. Clicking this button takes you directly to the login screen.

Step 3: Enter Your Credentials
------------------------------

The login window will prompt you to enter your account information. You will typically need to provide:

* **Registered Email Address**: Enter the email address you used when purchasing your subscription. This is the primary identifier for your account.
* **Password**: Enter the password you created during registration. Remember that passwords are case-sensitive, so ensure your Caps Lock key is not accidentally activated.

.. note::
   Some versions of Advanced SystemCare may offer alternative login methods:

   * **Social Login**: Sign in quickly using your Google, Facebook, or other social media accounts. This option links your social media account to your IObit account for faster access.
   * **One-Time Passcode**: Receive a temporary code via email for secure, password-free access. This is useful if you have forgotten your password or are logging in from a trusted device.
   * **License Code Only**: Enter your license code directly without creating or logging into a full account. This method is convenient for users who prefer not to maintain an online account.

Step 4: Complete the Login Process
----------------------------------

After entering your credentials, click the **Login**, **Sign In**, or **Register Now** button to proceed. The software will securely send your information to IObit servers for verification.

During the verification process, ensure your internet connection remains stable. The software will check the validity of your credentials, confirm your subscription status, and retrieve your license details.

Upon successful verification, you will receive confirmation that you are logged in. The main dashboard will update to reflect your premium status, typically displaying a "PRO" badge or similar indicator. All premium features will become available for immediate use.

What to Expect After Successful Login
-------------------------------------

Once you have successfully logged in, you will notice several changes in the software:

* **Premium Status Indicator**: A visual indicator confirms you are using the premium version.
* **Full Feature Access**: All previously locked features become available, including real-time protection, automatic driver updates, and advanced cleanup tools.
* **Subscription Details**: Your subscription plan, expiration date, and device limit are displayed in the account section.
* **Automatic Updates**: The software will check for and install updates that may have been unavailable in the free version.

How to Login Using a License Code
=================================

If you prefer not to create a full account or are activating the software for the first time, you can log in using your license code alone. This method is quick and does not require an email and password combination.

Step 1: Open Advanced SystemCare
--------------------------------

Launch the software on your computer.

Step 2: Navigate to License Activation
--------------------------------------

Click the **Options icon (⋮)** in the upper right corner and select **My Subscription** from the dropdown menu. Alternatively, look for the **Enter Code** link on the main dashboard.

Step 3: Enter Your License Code
-------------------------------

In the registration window, locate the field for entering your license code. This is typically a combination of letters and numbers. Carefully type or paste your license code into the field. Ensure there are no extra spaces before or after the code.

Step 4: Activate Your License
-----------------------------

Click the **Register Now**, **Activate**, or **Apply** button. The software will connect to IObit servers, verify your license code, and activate your subscription. After successful activation, your premium features will be unlocked.

Important Considerations When Using License Code Login
------------------------------------------------------

* **No Account Management**: While this method activates the software, it does not create a full account. You will not have access to online account management features like viewing subscription details or managing devices.
* **Limited Device Management**: Without an account, managing multiple devices requires keeping track of your license code and manually activating each device.
* **Password Recovery Not Available**: If you lose your license code, you cannot recover it through the software alone. It is recommended to keep a copy of your license code in a safe place.
* **Upgrade to Full Account**: You can create a full account later by visiting the IObit website and registering with the same email address used for purchase.

Common Login Issues and How to Resolve Them
===========================================

Even with clear instructions, you may occasionally encounter issues when attempting to log in. Here are the most common problems and detailed solutions:

Forgotten Password
------------------

Forgetting your password is one of the most common login issues. Here is how to recover it:

1. On the login screen (either in the software or on the website), click the **Forgot Password?** link.
2. Enter your registered email address in the provided field.
3. Click **Submit** or **Send Reset Link**.
4. Check your email inbox for a message from IObit containing a password reset link.
5. If you do not see the email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder.
6. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.
7. Return to the login screen and enter your new password.

Invalid Email or Password Error
-------------------------------

If you receive an error stating that your credentials are invalid, consider these possibilities:

* **Typographical Errors**: Double-check that your email address is spelled correctly. Common mistakes include using the wrong domain (e.g., .com instead of .net) or misspelling your email name.
* **Case Sensitivity**: Passwords are case-sensitive. Ensure your Caps Lock key is turned off and that you are entering uppercase and lowercase letters correctly.
* **Multiple Accounts**: You may have created multiple accounts with different email addresses. Try any other email addresses you may have used.
* **Account Deletion**: If you have not logged in for an extended period, your account may have been deactivated. Contact IObit support for assistance.

Account Not Found
-----------------

If the system says your account does not exist, try these steps:

* **Confirm Email Address**: Make absolutely certain you are using the correct email address. If you purchased the software through a third-party retailer, your account might be associated with a different email.
* **Check Purchase Confirmation**: Look for your purchase confirmation email from IObit. This email will contain your registered email address and order details.
* **Try Password Recovery**: Even if the system says the account does not exist, try the password recovery process. Sometimes accounts exist but are not recognized due to system errors.
* **Create a New Account**: If you are certain you do not have an account, you may need to create one. However, if you have already purchased a subscription, you should not create a new account. Instead, contact support to link your license to the correct account.

License Code Already Used or Expired
------------------------------------

If your license code shows as already used, consider these possibilities:

* **Already Activated**: The license code may have already been activated on this computer. Try logging in with your account credentials instead of entering the code again.
* **Device Limit Reached**: Your subscription may have a device limit, and you have reached it. You will need to deactivate an old device before activating a new one.
* **Expired Subscription**: Your subscription may have expired. Check your account dashboard for renewal options.
* **Fraudulent Use**: If you believe your license code has been used without your authorization, contact IObit support immediately.

Software Update Issues
----------------------

Sometimes login issues are caused by outdated software versions. To resolve this:

1. Ensure you are using the latest version of Advanced SystemCare.
2. Go to the official IObit website and download the latest installer.
3. Install the new version over your existing installation (your settings and data will be preserved).
4. Try logging in again.

Internet Connection Problems
----------------------------

A stable internet connection is essential for login. If you are having connection issues:

1. Verify your computer is connected to the internet by opening a web browser and loading a website.
2. If you are using Wi-Fi, try switching to a wired connection.
3. Restart your router and modem.
4. Disable VPN or proxy services temporarily.
5. Check if a firewall or security software is blocking Advanced SystemCare's connection.

Firewall or Antivirus Interference
----------------------------------

Other security software can sometimes interfere with Advanced SystemCare's login process. To resolve this:

1. Temporarily disable your firewall and antivirus software.
2. Try logging in again.
3. If successful, add Advanced SystemCare to your security software's exception list.
4. Re-enable your security software.

Corrupted Software Installation
-------------------------------

If the software itself is corrupted, you may encounter login issues. To fix this:

1. Uninstall Advanced SystemCare completely.
2. Restart your computer.
3. Download the latest version from the official IObit website.
4. Install the software fresh.
5. Attempt to log in again.

Troubleshooting Before Contacting Support
=========================================

Before reaching out to IObit customer support, work through this comprehensive troubleshooting checklist. Many login issues can be resolved without external assistance:

1. **Restart Your Computer**: A simple restart can resolve temporary system conflicts and clear memory issues that may be interfering with the login process.

2. **Check Your Internet Connection**: As mentioned previously, a stable internet connection is required. Run a quick test by visiting a website in your browser.

3. **Verify System Date and Time**: Incorrect system date or time settings can cause authentication errors. Ensure your computer's date, time, and time zone are set correctly.

4. **Update Advanced SystemCare**: Install the latest version of the software to ensure compatibility and access to the latest features.

5. **Clear Temporary Files**: Use Advanced SystemCare's own system cleaning tools or Windows Disk Cleanup to remove temporary files that may be causing conflicts.

6. **Disable Other Security Software**: Temporarily turn off other antivirus or firewall programs to test if they are blocking Advanced SystemCare.

7. **Run as Administrator**: Right-click the Advanced SystemCare shortcut and select **Run as Administrator**. This gives the software the permissions it may need to access system resources.

8. **Check for Windows Updates**: Ensure your operating system is up to date. Missing system updates can sometimes cause software compatibility issues.

9. **Try a Different Login Method**: If you are using the software to log in, try the website instead, or vice versa. This can help determine if the issue is specific to one platform.

10. **Use a Different Network**: If possible, try logging in from a different network (e.g., mobile hotspot) to rule out network-specific issues.

Best Practices for Account Security
===================================

Maintaining the security of your Advanced SystemCare account protects your subscription and personal information. Follow these best practices to keep your account secure:

Create a Strong Password
------------------------

Your password is your first line of defense. Create a password that is:

* At least 12 characters long.
* A mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters (e.g., !@#$%^&*).
* Not easily guessable (avoid common words, names, or personal information).
* Unique to your IObit account (do not reuse passwords from other websites).

Enable Two-Factor Authentication
--------------------------------

If IObit offers two-factor authentication (2FA), enable it. 2FA requires a second verification step, such as a code sent to your phone or email, in addition to your password. This significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access.

Never Share Your Credentials
----------------------------

Never share your login credentials with anyone. IObit employees will never ask for your password. Be cautious of phishing attempts that ask for your account information.

Log Out on Shared Devices
-------------------------

If you log in on a public or shared computer, always log out when you are finished. This prevents others from accessing your account.

Monitor Your Account Activity
-----------------------------

Regularly check your account dashboard for unfamiliar devices or unusual activity. If you see anything suspicious, change your password immediately and contact IObit support.

Update Your Software Regularly
------------------------------

Keep Advanced SystemCare and your operating system updated. Updates often include security patches that protect against vulnerabilities.

Beware of Phishing Scams
------------------------

Be cautious of emails or messages claiming to be from IObit that ask for your personal information. Always navigate to the official IObit website directly rather than clicking links in suspicious emails.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to login to Advanced SystemCare?

Is my account information secure?

How do I deactivate a device I no longer use?

How do I contact IObit support?

-------------------------------------------------------------

A license code is a unique alphanumeric string that activates the software. An account is a user profile that stores your license codes, subscription details, and device information. You can have multiple license codes under one account.
